How It Works

ClaimRight helps you reconnect with your lost items or return items you’ve found in a secure and easy way.

Report a Lost Item

Submit details like description, category, location, date, and photos. Your report will be listed in our Lost Items section.

Report a Found Item

Post details of the item you’ve found. It will appear in our Found Items section for the rightful owner to claim.

Claim an Item

Owners can prove ownership by describing unique details or showing evidence. Once verified, arrange a safe return.

Safety First

Use our secure messaging system, avoid oversharing personal details, and meet in safe public places for exchanges.

General Guidelines

  • Be honest and detailed in your reports.
  • Don’t post sensitive personal information publicly.
  • Always arrange item exchanges in safe locations.
  • Contact support if you face any issues.
